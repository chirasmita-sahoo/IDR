About IDR

The Institute of Digital Risk is an industry-led training and deployment institute focused on digital, cyber, and technology risk roles in regulated, high-consequence environments.

We emphasize the critical link between academic insight—through our UK university partnerships—and real-world projects to prepare the next generation of practitioners.

Academy

Industry-led training and bootcamps for students and professionals looking to enter the digital risk space.

Innovation & Incubation

Developing intellectual property, future risk models, and robust AI governance frameworks.

Advisory Services

Helping organizations implement industry-standard frameworks like NIST, ISO 27001, and NIS2.

Who We Serve

Our community consists of students, early-career professionals, and practitioners in sectors like financial services and critical infrastructure seeking to upskill in digital and cyber risk.
